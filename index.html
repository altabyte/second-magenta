Second Magenta

Second Magenta was started in 1997. Initially our contracts focused on updating key software to work in modern computer environments. Early success led to the development of a unique suite of modelling and testing software. These are now used by a number of leading aircraft windshield manufacturers across the world.

Business planning and market research skills led to further contract work as new clients and customers became aware of our company. Our particular strength in the support of new company start-ups was recognised locally and further growth ensured.

Second Magenta is now recognised by both large and small companies around the world as having a wealth of specialised knowledge and experience that enables them to solve their business problems.
