Second Magenta

Second Magenta was started in 1997. Initially our contracts focused on updating key software to work in the modern graphical environment. Early success led to the development of a unique suite of modelling and test software. These are now used by a number of leading aircraft windscreen manufacturers across the world.

Business planning and market research skills led to further contract work as new clients and customers became aware of our company. A particular strength in the support of new company start ups was recognised locally and further growth ensued.

Second Magenta is now recognised by both large and small companies across the world as having a wealth of specialised knowledge and experience that enables them to solve their business problems.
