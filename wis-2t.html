WIS 2T Modeller

Windshield 2T Modeller (WIS 2T) is a software tool to assist in the design of heated windscreens for aircraft and locomotive applications. WIS 2T brings together two separate design functions into one software package.

  • Anti-icing performance

    WIS 2T predicts the power density requirements for anti-icing with electrically heated windscreens in accordance with both SAE AIR1168/4 and AFML-TR-73-126 (Letton).

  • Thermal performance

    WIS 2T predicts the steady-state and dynamic temperature distribution throughout a coated laminated windscreen based on user-definable environment.
